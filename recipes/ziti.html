Chef John's Baked Ziti

Description

This is Chef John's Baked Ziti recipe. It's really easy, and makes lots of food. So what? No Ziti now? Yes, Ziti Now!

Ingredients

  • 1.5lb italian sausage - all hot, all sweet, a mix, it's up to you
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • .5tsp dried oregano
  • .25tsp dried thyme
  • .25tsp dried rosemary
  • 2 24oz jars marinara sauce - get Rao's
  • 2c water
  • 1 16oz pack ziti. you can use rigatoni or penne or something else short and round, but then it ain't ziti.
  • 1.5c whole-milk ricotta
  • 8oz mozzarella. not the fresh fancy kind, the industrial stuff.
  • 1c freshly grated parmesan/pecorino. this time you want the fancy stuff.
  • 1 bunch italian parsley
